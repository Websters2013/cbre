Services

CBRE Workspace offers an extensive range of services. Our consultants supports their clients in the definition of their real estate strategy : optimization of the space used, office refurbishment, definition and realization of a new work environment, relocation assistance.

workplace strategy

Supervision of the project owner in the thinking process, the design and implementation of the new way of working (functionality, image, well-being of users, optimal use of the surface).

ADVICE ON THE WORK ENVIRONMENT

Feasibility studies to assess the feasibility of upcoming projects by studying all the parameters of a project (technical, financial, HR, timings).

Programming of the future workspaces. The gathering of requirements according to the project owner with the aim of defining a target surface, including a possible future flexibility of the workspace.

SPACE PLANNING

At every stage of the project, from the feasibility study to the realization of the detailed plan, our interior architects graphically illustrate all the fit outs of the project.

Design

interior design

Realization of the entire interior design of the project. From the first sketches to a detailed presentation with moodboards (samples) and 3D views.

Decoration

Our specialist teams offer the best suited decoration taken into account the specifications of the project.

The furniture process could be integrated at every stage of the interior design, the integration on drawing until the delivery and installation on site.

Realisation of the fit-out

Project management of design and/or implementation

Design and/or implementation of a fit out project, lot by lot, with obligation of needs. Management and reporting of fit out project in consultation with the client. We ensure the coordination of all takeholders and project consistency. We also identify the critical points and perform the necessary actions throughout the project.

General contractor

Design and implementation of a turnkey project with guarantee of results.

Sustainability

Integrate the environmental requirements to the fit-out projects : HQE, BREEAM and LEED.

Create efficient workspaces and workspaces, healthy and comfortable : our interior designs support all the comfort and health requirements (visual comfort, noise insulation, air quality …).

Reduce the disruption and nuisance caused by our operations to the surrounding neighborhood : our methodology encourages the respect of the “green” precepts (waste sorting, less noise pollution, improved neighborhood communication, …).
